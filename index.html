About Nordic Barista Cup

The vision within the Nordic Barista Cup is:

"To create an environment in which knowledge about coffee and its shere cab be obtained"

"...create an environment..."
Combined with personally absorbtion, having the opportunity to see and experience countries, people, traditions, etc will always serve as a source of inspiration in our daily work.
The organisation behind the Nordic Barista Cup see it as its main purpose to be a part of creating this forum in which people can meet, bond and achieve further knowledge.
